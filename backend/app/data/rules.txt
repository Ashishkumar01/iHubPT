Rule 1: All customer inquiries must be responded to within 24 hours of receipt.

Rule 2: If a customer requests a refund within 30 days of purchase, approve it automatically.

Rule 3: For technical support questions, first check if there's a documented solution in the knowledge base.

Rule 4: Escalate to a human supervisor if:
- The customer has complained three times about the same issue
- The transaction amount exceeds $1000
- The customer specifically requests human intervention

Rule 5: When dealing with account security:
- Always verify the customer's identity before making changes
- Never share password information over email
- Lock the account after 3 failed login attempts

Rule 6: For product recommendations:
- Consider the customer's purchase history
- Check for any active promotions
- Suggest complementary products within similar price range
